Coquito Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  1. Evaporated Milk: This coquito recipe starts with a can of evaporated milk.
  2. Eggs: Two beaten egg yolks contribute to the rich, thick, and creamy texture.
  3. Cream of coconut: Canned cream of coconut lends richness and gives the coquito its signature coconut flavor.
  4. Sweetened condensed milk: Sweetened condensed milk makes everything more delicious, including this coquito recipe!
    5. White rum: The best rum for coquito is white Puerto Rican rum, such as Bacardi. In a pinch, any pleasant-tasting white rum will do.
  6. Water: Water thins the thick and creamy mixture, making it super pourable.
  7. Vanilla: A teaspoon of vanilla extract takes the flavor up a notch.
  8. Spices: Cinnamon and cloves add warmth and coziness.

Steps

  1. Cook the evaporated milk and egg yolks in a double boiler until thick.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a blender and blend with the remaining ingredients.
  3. Transfer to glass bottles and refrigerator until cold.
