Meatball Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound lean ground beef
  2. ½ cup bread crumbs
  3. 3 tablespoons minced onion
  4. 2 tablespoons water
  5. 1 large egg
  6. 1 (8 ounce) can jellied cranberry sauce
  7. ¾ cup chili sauce
  8. 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  9. 1 ½ teaspoons lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, bread crumbs, onion, water, and egg using your hands. Roll into small meatballs.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, turning once.
  4. In a slow cooker or large saucepan over low heat, blend the cranberry sauce, chili sauce, brown sugar, and lemon juice. Add meatballs, and simmer for 1 hour before serving.
